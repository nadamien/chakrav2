About Chakra Ella

Our Story

Born from a shared vision to create a haven of peace and purpose, Chakra Ella was founded on the belief that true well-being comes from living in harmony with nature.

"Chakra" represents the flow of life energy, balance, healing, and awakening. "Ella" reflects the surrounding hills and waterfalls that nurture our land. Together, they form a place where the body, mind, and spirit can realign.

Our Vision

To be a sanctuary for conscious travellers seeking connection with self, with nature, and with the community that sustains both.

Our Mission

To offer authentic, regenerative experiences that restore balance to people and the planet. We honour the natural environment, empower local communities, and welcome guests into a space built on respect, mindfulness, and love.

Our Philosophy

Every element at Chakra Ella, from our architecture to our meals and yoga sessions, flows with purpose. We utilise local materials, preserve natural habitats, and celebrate Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage. Guests are encouraged to slow down, breathe deeply, and remember the beauty of simplicity.

Local Attractions

Discover the beauty beyond our gates

Located between Ella and Wellawaya, Chakra Ella is perfectly placed for exploration. Within a short drive or scenic walk, guests can experience the island's most stunning natural and cultural landmarks.

Diyaluma Falls

Sri Lanka's second-highest waterfall is perfect for a dip and breathtaking views.

Ella Rock

A scenic hike with panoramic mountain views.

Ravana Falls & Cave Temple

Steeped in legend and natural beauty.

Nine Arches Bridge

Iconic railway bridge surrounded by tea gardens.

Buduruwagala Temple

Ancient rock carvings of Buddha, hidden among the trees.

Lipton's Seat

Historic tea estate viewpoint, ideal for sunrise lovers.

Mini Adam's Peak

An easy but rewarding hike with incredible scenery.
