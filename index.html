Our Story

Reconnect with Nature

Chakra Ella is more than just a resort; it's a sanctuary where luxury and sustainability coexist in perfect harmony.

Nestled in the pristine wilderness of Sri Lanka, our eco-luxury glamping resort offers an unparalleled experience of comfort amidst nature. Each of our thoughtfully designed tents provides panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, allowing you to wake up to the sounds of exotic birds and the rustling of leaves.

We believe in responsible tourism that preserves the natural beauty of our surroundings while providing our guests with exceptional comfort and unforgettable experiences. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in every aspect of our operations, from solar-powered facilities to locally sourced organic cuisine.
